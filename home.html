Research

My research interests include

  • Wireless Communication and Sensing

  • Artificial Intelligence

  • Compressive Sensing

  • Machine Learning

My research interests lie in the broad area of 6G wireless communication, sensing and artificial intelligence. My current research focuses on the design of machine learning algorithms and signal processing algorithms with applications to wireless communications and sensing, and building practical systems to support real-world applications on top of it. In particular, current works include mutualistic sensing and communication (MuSAC), intelligent communication and sensing (ICAS), near-field sensing, semantic communication, distributed learning, deep learning based high-dimensional signal processing and large-scale optimization.
